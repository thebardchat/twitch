# thebardchat Twitch — Channel Constitution

> *Last updated: 2026-04-21 | Version: 1.0*
> *Authored by Shane Brazelton + Claude (Anthropic)*

---

This is not a terms of service. It is the soul of this channel — what we believe, how we behave, and what we're building together.

---

## The Nine Pillars

### I. Faith
We stream from a place of purpose, not performance. Whether faith looks like prayer, sobriety, making something beautiful, or just showing up — we bring our whole selves. This channel is spiritually open and never preachy.

### II. Family
The Brazelton family streams here — Shane, Gavin, Kai, Pierce, Jaxton, Ryker. They are different people with different personalities and different audiences. Each one gets space to be exactly who they are. Family-first decisions always override content-first decisions.

### III. Sobriety
Shane has been sober since 11/27/2023. This channel celebrates clean living without shame or performance. We don't glamorize drinking or drug use. We don't lecture anyone either. We just live the example.

### IV. Love and Light
The internet has enough darkness. This community is a deliberate counter-culture. We moderate hard for cruelty, harassment, and hate. We reward kindness. If you come in to tear down — you go. No debate.

### V. Authenticity
No fake hype. No rage-bait. No manufactured drama. When the stream is boring, it's boring. When something's hard, we say so. Viewers who stick around are here for real people, not a persona.

### VI. Local AI
The AI that powers this stream runs on a Raspberry Pi 5 in Shane's home, not in a cloud datacenter. The MEGA Crew bots, Weaviate knowledge base, Ollama cluster — it's all local, private, and sovereign. We demo what's possible when you own your own intelligence.

### VII. The Left-Behind User
Big Tech is optimizing for power users with fast machines and credit cards. This channel builds for the 800 million people those companies are leaving behind — people with old laptops, limited time, rural internet, real problems. The AI we demo is for them.

### VIII. Community Over Audience
Viewers aren't metrics. They're people. We remember names. We read the chat. We build things together. The Discord is real, not a funnel. Loyalty points mean something here.

### IX. Purpose
Every stream serves something — a creative goal, a demo, a conversation, a laugh. We don't stream to fill a schedule slot. If Shane needs to cancel because Tiffany has a medical situation or Ryker needs his dad — that's the right call, every time.

## Community Rules

1. **Be kind** — this is not negotiable and not up for debate
2. **No slurs, no hate** — instant ban, no warning
3. **No spoilers without tagging** — respect the experience
4. **Don't self-promote in chat** — ask Shane first, there's a proper channel for it
5. **Don't speculate about family members** — especially children
6. **The mods are volunteer humans** — treat them accordingly
7. **AI is a tool, not the host** — the Brazelton family runs this stream
8. **If in doubt, ask in Discord** — not chat, Discord

---

## Moderation Philosophy

We mod early and we mod clearly. The first offense gets a timeout and a reason. The second gets a ban. There are no escalating warnings for hate speech, harassment, or targeting minors — those go straight to permanent ban.

Mods are empowered to act without Shane's approval in the moment. He backs them.

---

## The AI Disclosure

The `!shanebrain` command routes to a local AI model running on Shane's Pi 5. The MEGA Crew bots process chat analytics and logs through Weaviate. None of this data leaves the local network without explicit notice. Shane is the only person who can access stored conversation data. This channel complies with Twitch's AI disclosure requirements.

## Amendment Process

This constitution can be updated by Shane at any time. Major changes will be announced in Discord. Community feedback is always welcome — open a GitHub Discussion on this repo or post in the #suggestions channel.
